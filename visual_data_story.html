Analyzing Daily Weather Patterns

Daily weather

This project explores the changes in weather over a course of 40 days (March 28th to May 6th). By manually logging the weather conditions, temperature, and sunset time, the goal is to visualize changes and patterns of the weather.

The plots below visualize: - How does the temperature vary? - Which weather types were most common? - Is there a trend between sunset time and changing weather?

Weather types

The most recorded weather was cloudy.

How Did Temperature Change Over Time?

Temperatures started relatively high around 23C, then gradually decreased as the season became more close to winter.

Average Temperature by Weather Condition

On average, Clear/Sunny conditions had the highest temperatures, while Rainy weather tended to be cooler. Windy conditions were surprisingly warm as well.
